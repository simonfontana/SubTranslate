# Privacy Policy

SubTranslate is a browser extension that translates subtitle text on video streaming sites using the DeepL API.

## Data collected by the developer

None. The developer does not collect, receive, or have access to any data from users of this extension.

## Data handled locally

The following data is stored locally on your device using `browser.storage.local` and never transmitted to the developer:

- **DeepL API key** — entered by you in the extension popup, used solely to authenticate requests to your own DeepL account.
- **Extension settings** — your preferences as configured in the extension popup.

## Data sent to third parties

When you click on subtitle text, the selected word or sentence is sent to the **DeepL API** (`api.deepl.com` or `api-free.deepl.com`) for translation. This request is made using your own DeepL API key and is subject to [DeepL's privacy policy](https://www.deepl.com/en/privacy).

No other data is sent anywhere.

## Permissions

- **`storage`** — to save your settings (API key, language preferences, font size) locally on your device.
- **Host permissions for `api.deepl.com` and `api-free.deepl.com`** — to send translation requests to the DeepL API on your behalf.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same location.
